biography

- computer information systems (PCC, 2017)
- cybersecurity (PCC, in process)
- data science (UCSD/edx, 2020)
- planned: cisco, microsoft, comptia, ETS & GRE physics
- python, C, java, javascript, XML

i work with ableton, neural network/midi generator freeware, a yamaha tenori-on, and a mimu glove. my music is self-released through cdbaby, i'm represented by ASCAP and songtrust, and a member of UMAW.

physics hobbyist and amateur enthusiast. pursuing cosmology phd (applying soon). my interest is in static redshift models, i think leaving out expansion and inflation (or recharacterizing them as dynamic gravity) might be a promising avenue to new physics. this is the "modified newtonian dynamics" corner of physics, although i prefer broader terms like modified gravity. i try to keep up with CERN, neutrino observatories, and LIGO.

fun facts. um. i studied theater for undergrad (university of utah), and i was a background extra in high school musical and halloweentown 3 (halloweentown high). i was born in 1989 in utah and i am much gayer than anyone ever thought it was possible to be.
