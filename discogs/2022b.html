pillowtalk

  • release date: 2/11/22
  • tenori-on, mimu, focusrite scarlett 2i2, ableton, audacity

sort of a studio set or live album, except in the sense that the studio is my bedroom, there was no audience, and i did a bit of post-production editing.

toshio iwai's tenori-on has a unique signature sound, one of the reasons i keep coming back to it. i've added some 808 samples, but unfortunately i loaded these so long ago i'm not sure where they're from (freesound.org, probably). every other sound is from the tenori-on's internal synthesizer.
