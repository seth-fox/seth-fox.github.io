album info and errata

these pages contain some background on the ideas behind each of the albums i've put together, what i was thinking about and working with at the time. you know, liner notes. i'd like to add some scrapbook photos of the places i was living when i made these, or what i looked like at the time, but it will take a bit of digging for me to put those together.

most of my work is made in ableton, and about half of it it using a yamaha tenori'on. each of my albums are usually also made working with some type of code-based midi generator freeware. (people variously call these machine learning algorithms or neural networks or artificial intelligence, but some of the midi generators i've used are much simpler.)
