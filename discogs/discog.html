intro to midi generator music (essay)


album info

spooky dark cerebral academic instrumental electronic, mostly.

ideas behind each of the albums i've put together, what i was thinking about & working with at the time. you know, liner notes. i'd like to add some scrapbook photos of the places i was living when i made these, or what i looked like at the time, but it will take a bit of digging for me to put those together.

most of my work is made with ableton, yamaha cvp-307, & tenori-on. each album is usually made by working with some type of midi generator freeware. people variously call these machine learning algorithms or neural networks or artificial intelligence, but some of the midi generators i've used are much simpler.
